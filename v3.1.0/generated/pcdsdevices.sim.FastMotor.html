pcdsdevices.sim.FastMotor¶

class pcdsdevices.sim.FastMotor(*args, init_pos=0, **kwargs)¶

Instant motor with FltMvInterface.

This is suitable to replace real motors in the PseudoPositioner subclasses. It does not have all of the SynAxis functionality.
