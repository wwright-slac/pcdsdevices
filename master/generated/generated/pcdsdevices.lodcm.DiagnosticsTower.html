pcdsdevices.lodcm.DiagnosticsTower¶

class pcdsdevices.lodcm.DiagnosticsTower(prefix, *args, **kwargs)¶

LODCM Diagnostic Tower.

Located midway between Crystal Tower 1 and Crystal Tower 2, in the center of rotation of the device. It contains a pips diode(dd), yag screen(dv), filters wheel (df) and vertical (dh) and horizontal slits (dd).

Parameters

  • prefix (str) – Epics base PV prefix.

  • name (str) – The name of this device.
