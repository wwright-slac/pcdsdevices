pcdsdevices.ccm.CCMEnergy

class pcdsdevices.ccm.CCMEnergy(*args, **kwargs)

CCM energy motor.

Calculates the current CCM energy using the alio position, and requests moves to the alio based on energy requests.

Presents itself like a motor.

Parameters

prefix (str) – The PV prefix of the Alio motor, e.g. XPP:MON:MPZ:07A
